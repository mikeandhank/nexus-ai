# NexusOS Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 16, 2026

## 1. Introduction

NexusOS ("we," "our," or "us") is a self-hosted AI agent platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

**Key Point:** Because NexusOS is self-hosted, your data stays on YOUR infrastructure by default. We have no access to your data unless you explicitly share it.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email, username, password (hashed)
- **Agent Configurations:** System prompts, settings
- **Conversations:** Chat history with agents (stored locally)

### Automatically Collected
- **Usage Data:** API calls, response times, feature usage
- **System Logs:** Error logs, performance metrics (anonymized)

### NOT Collected
- We do NOT collect your chat content
- We do NOT access your agents
- We do NOT monitor your conversations

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve the Service
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Analyze usage patterns for product improvement
- Send service-related notifications

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- **Local Deployment:** When you self-host NexusOS, data stays on your servers
- **Encryption:** Data at rest can be encrypted (optional feature)
- **Security:** We implement industry-standard security measures

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties, except:
- With your explicit consent
- To comply with legal requirements
- To service providers who assist our operations

## 6. Third-Party Services

- **LLM Providers:** If you use cloud LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic), their privacy policies apply
- **Hosting Providers:** If hosted externally, their security practices apply

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Object to processing
- Data portability

## 8. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Conversation history: Until you delete it
- Usage logs: Retained for 90 days for analytics

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes.
