# Marketing — Case Studies

## 5 Successes

### 1. Slack

**What happened:** Grew to millions without ads.

**Core insight:**
- Product-led growth
- Word-of-mouth from great product
- Bottom-up adoption

**What it teaches:**
- Product is marketing
- Organic growth compounds

---

### 2. HubSpot

**What happened:** Built $25B+ company on content.

**Core insight:**
- Inbound marketing methodology
- SEO as growth engine
- Education as lead gen

**What it teaches:**
- Content compounds
- Methodology creates category

---

### 3. Dropbox

**What happened:** Grew with referral program.

**Core insight:**
- Give storage, get storage
- Viral loop built in
- Low CAC referral

**What it teaches:**
- Referral is powerful
- Incentives drive behavior

---

### 4. Dollar Shave Club

**What happened:** $1B exit with one video.

**Core insight:**
- Funny, memorable content
- Distribution via YouTube
- Clear value proposition

**What it teaches:**
- Viral content works
- Simplicity wins

---

### 5. Apple

**What happened:** Brand as competitive advantage.

**Core insight:**
- Premium positioning
- Emotional connection
- Product launches as events

**What it teaches:**
- Brand has value
- Simplicity in messaging
