# Marketing — Foundations

## Core Definition

Marketing is the function of communicating value to customers. It includes everything from product positioning to pricing to channels to messaging. The goal: reach the right audience with the right message at the right time.

---

## The 4 Ps

| P | Description |
|---|-------------|
| **Product** | What you're selling |
| **Price** | How much it costs |
| **Place** | Where it's sold (channels) |
| **Promotion** | How you communicate (messaging) |

---

## Marketing Channels

| Channel | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Content/SEO** | Organic, educational content |
| **Paid ads** | PPC, social ads, display |
| **Email** | Direct communication |
| **Social** | Organic social, influencer |
| **Referral** | Word-of-mouth, programs |
| **Sales** | Direct sales, SDRs |

---

## Funnel Stages

1. **Awareness** — They know you exist
2. **Interest** — They engage
3. **Consideration** — They evaluate
4. **Conversion** — They buy
5. **Retention** — They stay
6. **Advocacy** — They refer

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value |
| **Conversion Rate** | % who move to next stage |
| **ROI** | Return on Marketing Investment |
| **NPS** | Net Promoter Score |

---

## Core Principles

1. **Message to market match**
2. **Test channels before scaling**
3. **Content builds organic presence**
4. **Email is owned channel**
5. **Referral is cheapest acquisition**
