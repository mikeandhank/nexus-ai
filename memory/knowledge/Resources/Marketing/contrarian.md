# Marketing — Contrarian Views

## What Most People Get Wrong

### 1. "More channels = more customers"

**Wrong:** Be everywhere.

**Reality:**
- Focus wins
- Master one channel before expanding
- CAC explodes with too many channels

### 2. "Paid ads solve everything"

**Wrong:** Just run more ads.

**Reality:**
- Organic compounds, paid decays
- ROAS may not be sustainable
- Dependence on platforms

### 3. "Content is free"

**Wrong:** Organic content is cheap.

**Reality:**
- Content takes time/resources
- Quality content is expensive
- Compounding takes time

### 4. "Branding doesn't matter"

**Wrong:** Focus on performance.

**Reality:**
- Brand drives long-term CAC
- Trust enables conversion
- Differentiation matters

### 5. "Email is dead"

**Wrong:** Social is where people are.

**Reality:**
- Email has highest ROI
- Owned channel, no algorithm
- Direct access to customers

## Key Insights

| What Fails | What Works |
|------------|------------|
| Scattergun channels | Focused execution |
| Paid dependency | Organic compounding |
| Ignore brand | Brand building |
