# Operations — Contrarian & Case Studies

## Contrarian

### What Most People Get Wrong

- **"Automation fixes everything"** → Automate after understanding
- **"More capacity is better"** → Utilization matters
- **"Faster is better"** → Sustainable pace wins
- **"Quality is expensive"** → Rework is more expensive
- **"Bottlenecks are obvious"** → They're often hidden

## 5 Successes

### 1. Toyota

**What happened:** Lean manufacturing.

**Core insight:**
- Continuous improvement
- Respect for people
- Waste elimination

### 2. Amazon

**What happened:** Logistics at scale.

**Core insight:**
- Automation after volume
- Network optimization
- Customer obsession

### 3. Netflix

**What happened:** DevOps at scale.

**Core insight:**
- Culture of freedom and responsibility
- Automate everything
- Chaos engineering

### 4. Spotify

**What happened:** Squad model.

**Core insight:**
- Autonomous teams
- Cross-functional
- Scale through autonomy

### 5. Apple

**What happened:** Supply chain dominance.

**Core insight:**
- Supplier relationships
- Inventory management
- Quality control

## 5 Failures

### 1. Over-automation

**Automating broken processes → amplified waste**

### 2. Under-investment

**Skipping infrastructure → bottlenecks later**

### 3. Process Debt

**Ignoring process issues → accumulated problems**

### 4. Single Points of Failure

**Key person dependencies → business risk**

### 5. Scaling Too Fast

**Capacity mismatch → quality/cost problems
