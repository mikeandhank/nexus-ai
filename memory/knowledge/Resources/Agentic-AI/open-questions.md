# Agentic AI — Open Questions

## Known Unknowns

These are questions I cannot answer confidently yet. I will return to them as I learn more.

---

### 1. What is the optimal architecture for a given business problem?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Too many variables: problem type, scale, integration requirements, budget
- No standard framework for choosing architecture
- Different frameworks excel at different things

**What would change my mind:**
- Comparative analysis of architectures by use case
- Framework evaluation criteria
- Case studies of architecture decisions and outcomes

---

### 2. How do you measure agentic AI ROI accurately?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Many implementations use vague metrics
- Hard to isolate AI impact from other factors
- Long-term vs. short-term ROI different

**What would change my mind:**
- Standardized ROI frameworks
- Data from implementations with proven ROI
- Metrics that isolate AI contribution

---

### 3. What governance framework works for autonomous agents?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Best practices are still emerging
- Regulatory landscape is unsettled
- Industry-specific requirements vary

**What would change my mind:**
- Proven governance frameworks from regulated industries
- Regulatory guidance (when it emerges)
- Case studies of governance failures

---

### 4. How do agents handle edge cases in high-stakes decisions?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Most implementations avoid high-stakes
- Edge cases by definition are rare and unpredictable
- No standard approach exists

**What would change my mind:**
- Research on edge case handling
- Frameworks for identifying high-stakes boundaries
- Case studies of edge case failures

### 5. What is the real total cost of ownership?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Most vendors don't disclose real operational costs
- Cost models vary wildly by use case
- Hidden costs (integration, monitoring, governance) are significant

**What would change my mind:**
- TCO analysis from implementations
- Vendor cost transparency
- Independent benchmarking

### 6. How will regulation affect agentic AI deployment?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Regulatory landscape is still forming

- Industry-specific regulations vary

**What would change my mind:**

### 7. What skills are needed to build and maintain agentic systems?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Skills gap is widely acknowledged but poorly quantified

**What would change my mind:**
- Industry skill surveys

### 8. How do multi-agent systems handle conflict resolution?

**Why I can't answer:**
- Most implementations avoid true multi-agent

**What would change my mind:**
- Research on multi-agent coordination
