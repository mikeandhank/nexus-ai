- How many agents included? What features?

### 2. Platform vs Point Solution
- Build a general platform or specialize in one vertical?
- Trade-off: TAM vs depth

### 3. Build vs Buy
- What do we build vs integrate?
- Stripe, Zapier, other APIs?
