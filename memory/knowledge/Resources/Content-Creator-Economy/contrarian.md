# Content & Creator Economy — Contrarian Views

## What Most People Get Wrong

### 1. "Monetize early"

**Wrong:** Build audience, then find ways to make money.

**Reality:**
- Monetization too early destroys trust
- Audience first, revenue second
- Quality of audience > quantity

### 2. "Platform is the business"

**Wrong:** Build large following on YouTube/TikTok.

**Reality:**
- Algorithm can change overnight
- Platform doesn't own the relationship
- Owned channels (newsletter, website) = true asset

### 3. "More content = more growth"

**Wrong:** Post more, grow faster.

**Reality:**
- Consistency beats volume
- Quality builds trust
- Algorithm rewards engagement, not just posting

### 4. "Any audience is valuable"

**Wrong:** Build the biggest audience possible.

**Reality:**
- Niche, engaged audience > mass, passive audience
- Per-fan revenue matters
- Superfans are the business

### 5. "Sponsorships are the goal"

**Wrong:** Land big brand deals.

**Reality:**
- Brand deals are unstable
- Direct revenue (subs, merch) is better
- Diversification is key

## Key Insights

| What Fails | What Works |
|------------|------------|
| Early monetization | Audience first |
| Platform dependency | Owned channels |
| Volume over quality | Consistency |
| Mass audience | Engaged niche |
| Single revenue stream | Diversification |
