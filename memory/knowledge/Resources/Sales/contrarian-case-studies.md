# Sales — Contrarian & Case Studies

## Contrarian

### What Most People Get Wrong

- **"Always be closing"** → Listen more than you talk
- **"More leads = more revenue"** → Quality beats quantity
- **"Discount to close"** → Value, not price
- **"Cold calling is dead"** → Still works for enterprise

## 5 Successes

### 1. Salesforce

**What happened:** Built enterprise sales machine.

**Core insight:**
- Land and expand
- Executive relationships
- Customer success drives expansion

### 2. HubSpot

**What happened:** Inbound sales motion.

**Core insight:**
- Leads find you
- Nurture before close
- Product qualifies leads

### 3. Gong

**What happened:** Data-driven sales.

**Core insight:**
- Record calls
- Analyze patterns
- Improve what works

### 4. Stripe

**What happened:** Developer sales.

**Core insight:**
- No sales team initially
- Product self-sells
- Documentation is marketing

### 5. Slack

**What happened:** Bottom-up enterprise.

**Core insight:**
- End users love it
- Grassroots adoption
- Champions advocate internally

## 5 Failures

### 1. Unsqualified Pursuit

**Chasing deals that won't close → wasted time, high CAC**

### 2. Discounting

**Reducing price to win → erodes margins, attracts wrong customers**

### 3. Poor Discovery

**Not understanding needs → wrong solution, churn**

### 4. No Follow-up

**Letting deals go cold → lost revenue**

### 5. Handoffs

**Sales to success failure → churn after close
