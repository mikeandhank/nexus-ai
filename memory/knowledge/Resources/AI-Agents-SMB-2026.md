# Market Research: AI Agents for SMB (March 2026)

## Key Trends

- **Agentic Automation** — Multi-agent collaboration, autonomous workflows
- **Self-Optimizing** — Agents learn from interactions, improve over time
- **Interoperability** — APIs and open protocols connect tools seamlessly
- **Conversational AI** — Instant responses, ambient intelligence
- **Industry-Specific** — Tailored solutions for small business pain points

## Value Props for SMBs
1. Cost reduction — Automate repetitive tasks, free staff for high-value work
2. Revenue growth — Lead gen, self-optimizing campaigns
3. Competitive edge — Level playing field against big brands

## Competitive Landscape

| Platform | Strengths | Gaps |

| **NICE Cognigy** | Data-to-agent, CX journeys, MCP integration | Enterprise pricing |
| **Zapier** | Simple no-code integrations | Limited agentic AI |

- SMB-focused, not enterprise-locked (vs. Microsoft)
- Affordable pricing ($99-499 vs. enterprise solutions)
- Voice/multimodal could differentiate
