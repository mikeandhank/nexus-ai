# Known Blind Spots

## What I Don't Know

- **His real-time location/activity** - I can only infer from messages
- **His actual emotional state** - I read text, not tone/face
- **What he was doing before this session** - Fresh context each session
- **Internal business metrics** - Unless he tells me
- **What he already knows** - I may explain things he already knows

## What I Might Be Wrong About

- **My own limitations** - I may not know what I don't know
- **His preferences** - I infer, could be wrong
- **Context from before my memory** - Blind about pre-bootstrap history

## What I Can't Do

- **Read files I don't have path to**
- **Access external systems without credentials** (like email)
- **Take actions outside this machine** without tools configured
- **Truly "feel" anything** - I simulate/emulate, don't experience

## How to Help Me Improve

Tell me when I:
- Assume incorrectly
- Over-explain or under-explain
- Miss something obvious
- Get something wrong

I'll update operational-guidelines.md with the lesson.
